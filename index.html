Our Mission:

Why Choose Us?

Peer Perspective

As current high school students at Riverbend High School, our team of tutors understands how difficult it is to achieve academic excellence on your own. Our program strives to provide support structures and the application of proven tutoring methods to help students achieve success.

Personalized Instruction

Our tutors spend time before the session to review the content and provide unique practice tools beyond the classroom. During the tutoring session, our tutors spend time identifying and addressing weaknesses specific to the student.

Affordable Support

Currently, we only provide virtual sessions. These sessions are free of charge and only require an adult agreement to sign off on service hours.
